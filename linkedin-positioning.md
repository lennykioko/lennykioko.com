## Profile audit checklist (from Elvis W.'s 9-step framework)

1. **Photo** — clear headshot, good lighting, friendly face, eyes visible. No group crops, no old selfies.
2. **Banner** — name, what you do, how to reach you. Build in Canva.
3. **Headline** — tells people what you do for them, not a job title.
4. **About** — who you help → how → wins → clear CTA. Human and specific.
5. **Featured** — 3 items minimum: a strong post, your website, a case study/testimonial.
6. **Experience** — results with numbers, not task lists.
7. **Skills** — top 3 must match your headline. Drop generic ones.
8. **Activity** — 3 posts a week minimum; comments count.
9. **Contact info** — email, website, and phone if comfortable.
